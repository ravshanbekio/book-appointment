Got Questions?

What information do I need to book an appointment?

You will need to provide your full name, phone number, and email address. Additionally, we require a brief description of the purpose of your appointment to ensure that we allocate the appropriate amount of time and resources.

Can I book the appointment online?

Yes, you can easily book the appointment online. Visit our website, navigate to the booking section, select your desired date and time, and fill out the required information. You will receive a confirmation once the booking is complete.

Is there a confirmation for the booked appointment?

Yes, after booking your appointment, you will receive an email confirmation. This confirmation will include the date, time, and location of your appointment, along with any other necessary details. If you prefer, we can also send a confirmation via SMS.

How far in advance can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment up to three months in advance. This allows you to plan ahead and secure a time that is convenient for you.

What payment methods do you accept for booking?

For any services that require payment, we accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. You can choose the most convenient payment method for you.

Is there a waiting list if no appointments are available?

Yes, if there are no available appointments, we can place you on a waiting list. We will notify you as soon as a slot opens up. This way, you have the opportunity to get an earlier appointment if someone else cancels.
